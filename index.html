Veg Briyani

Sauté onion in olive oil until translucent. Add garlic, ginger, tomatoes, and water; simmer 10 mins. Mix in chopped veggies and spices. Pour in broth, bring to boil. Add rinsed basmati rice, simmer covered on low for 18–20 mins. Let sit 5 mins before serving. Garnish with fresh coriander before serving. Serve hot with raita or pickle for added taste.

Hyderabadi Veg Biryani

Sauté whole spices, onion, ginger-garlic paste. Add chopped veggies, yogurt, mint, coriander, biryani masala, and cook. Parboil basmati rice with salt and whole spices. Layer rice over the veggie mix. Top with saffron milk, fried onions, mint, and ghee. Seal pot, cook on low (dum) for 20–25 mins.

Non-Veg Biryani

Marinate chicken with yogurt, spices, and lemon for 1 hour. Sauté onions, add marinated chicken, cook until 70% done. Parboil basmati rice with whole spices. Layer rice over chicken, top with fried onions, saffron milk, mint, coriander, and ghee. Seal pot and cook on dum (low heat) for 20–25 mins. Rest before serving.

Fried Rice

Heat oil in a pan and sauté garlic and onions until fragrant. Add chopped vegetables and cook on high heat until slightly tender. Add cooked rice, soy sauce, pepper, and salt. Stir-fry everything together for 2–3 minutes until well mixed and heated through. Serve hot for a quick delicious meal. Garnish with spring onions for extra flavor.
